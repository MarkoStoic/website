Who Am I?

The big question. I'm a 2-hearted, competitive, loving and sometimes lonely person.

I was born in Germany to proud immigrant Yugos. The first German we learned was in school. We travelled back to post-war Bosnia twice a year and it really felt like home. In Germany, I'm Bosnian. Abroad, I'm German.

I love competing. But my biggest competitor is myself. I don't know how healthy it is to think of your self in 3 tenses, but past-Marko is always an ass. Future-Marko is who I aspire to be, so present-Marko is always busy.

Truly loving is one of the scariest things I've ever done. Max potential to be hurt and yet you have to try. I honestly believe the reason we are on earth is to love and spread it.

Since I continously try to be a better version of myself, I also spend a lot of time working on myself. Improving some part of my routine, focusing more on nutrition or tracking my sleep. It all takes time and sometimes I forget that there is more to this world.

Ask me anything

What I'm Currently Working On

Traditionally, after NYE I always choose a challenge for that year but this year I went a bit more traditional and wrote down 4 things I'd like to do.

PS: Past challenges have been cold showers, NoFap and reading 2 books a month.

Start Building Wealth

It feels like this is the year to start making some proper money. Not sure if I want to move to a low-tax country, but I'll definitely be very active with my investments. Goal: 5x

Learn to Quickly Build Products

I'll found my own startup at some point and so far I've always failed because I couldn't build what I had imagined. ChatGPT might already be the answer to my cries but I really want to improve on this.

Build Atomic Habits

I'm great with routines. I keep stacking habits and they stick. But once I travel it all flies out of the window and kills all the progress I've made so far.

Start New Hobbies

Work, Girlfriend and Gym aren't hobbies. I want to explore Astronomy, Diving and Blogging as potential free time activities.
